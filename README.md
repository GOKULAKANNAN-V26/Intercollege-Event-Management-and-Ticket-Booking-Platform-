# Intercollege Event Management and Ticket Booking Platform

## Overview

The Intercollege Event Management and Ticket Booking Platform is a full-stack web application developed to digitize and automate the complete lifecycle of college event management. The system enables organizers to create and manage events, handle participant registrations, process online ticket bookings, verify attendees through QR codes, and monitor event performance through a centralized administrative dashboard.

The platform addresses common challenges associated with manual event management, including registration errors, ticket overbooking, inefficient participant tracking, payment reconciliation issues, and attendance management.

---

## Problem Statement

Traditional event management processes often rely on spreadsheets, manual registrations, and paper-based ticketing systems. These methods are time-consuming, prone to human error, and difficult to scale for large intercollege events.

The objective of this project is to provide a secure, scalable, and user-friendly digital platform that streamlines event operations while improving the experience for both participants and event organizers.

## System Workflow

1. Users register and verify their identity through OTP authentication.
2. Participants browse available events and select desired activities.
3. The system validates ticket availability before booking.
4. Secure online payment is processed through the payment gateway.
5. Upon successful payment, booking confirmation is generated.
6. A unique QR-based digital ticket is issued to the participant.
7. Event coordinators verify tickets through QR code scanning during entry.
8. Attendance and participation records are updated automatically.
